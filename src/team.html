CodeX Leadership and Roles

CodeX Initiative is not only about students developing software solutions to problems around the world, but is also about collaberation, leadership, and community building. We believe that by offering multiple leadership roles, we can provide opportunities for more students to develop their leadership skills and become more prepared for real world challenges.

Arnav Saraf - Founder and President

Arnav Saraf is the founder and president of CodeX Initiative. He has a passion for coding and programming, and he is committed to making a positive impact on the world through technology.

Arnav's favorite subjects include computer and data science, AI, physics, areospace engineering, and more. He loves reading and learning more about new science discoveries and innovations.

Arnav hopes that this organization can help inspire others to collaberate and solve real world problems.
